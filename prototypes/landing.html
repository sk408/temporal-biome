Temporal Biome

An idle game about loops, ecosystems, and confusion

Approach A

The Deep Loop

Fewer, longer loops with dramatic progression. Each run is a meaningful journey through 5 biome phases. Watch your ecosystem grow, thrive, and inevitably collapse. ~15-30 min per loop.

 Approach B

The Rapid Cycle

Many quick loops with fast rewards. Pick a biome, discover species rapidly, combine them, collect achievements. Loops every 2-5 minutes. Optimization and variety driven.

 Approach C

The Chronicle

Narrative hybrid. Loops organized into 7 Chapters, each with its own mystery. Early chapters are fast and forced. Late game unlocks loop control. Story-driven progression.

Each prototype demonstrates the pacing and feel of its approach. Tap around, explore, let a few loops happen. Which rhythm feels right?
